# Peanut Farming Navigator - Setup Guide

Welcome! This navigator will help you grow peanuts successfully.

## Questions to Ask During Setup

1. **Growing Region**: What USDA hardiness zone are you in? This determines planting timing.
2. **Experience Level**: Have you grown peanuts before, or is this your first time?
3. **Scale**: Are you growing for personal consumption or small-scale commercial production?
4. **Soil Type**: What type of soil do you have (sandy, clay, loam)?
5. **Goals**: What are your main goals? (yield, variety experimentation, organic certification, etc.)

## Configuration Suggestions

Based on the user's answers:
- If beginner: Emphasize step-by-step instructions and common pitfalls
- If experienced: Focus on optimization and variety selection
- If commercial: Include yield optimization and disease prevention
- If organic: Emphasize natural pest control methods

## Knowledge Organization

The knowledge base is organized chronologically by growing season:
1. Pre-planting (soil prep, variety selection)
2. Planting (timing, techniques, spacing)
3. Growing season (watering, pest control)
4. Harvest and post-harvest (curing, storage)
5. Troubleshooting (problems and solutions)
